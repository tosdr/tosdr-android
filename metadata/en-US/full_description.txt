"I agree to the Terms and Conditions of this Website".
The biggest lie of the internet. No one wants to spend an hour reading a wall of text. So we click "Accept" and move on, without knowing what we just agreed.
Let's make that a thing of the past!
With this app, you can search for Websites or Share an Website into it to get a Report of what is in the policies and it displays a Grade on Privacy, so you will actually know what you are agreeing on!
